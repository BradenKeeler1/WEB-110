About My Soccer Passion

I've been playing soccer for 16 years and have played in both the competitive and recreational scenes. My recent years have been my favorite because I feel like I’ve grown into a strong player and a solid leader. This year, I played for Fountain City FC and was the captain of the first team. It was an amazing season, and I met so many of my friends through that team.

Playing soccer has taught me teamwork, discipline, and leadership. It helped me grow out of my introverted ways and pushed me to become more confident and outgoing.

Future

My future in soccer isn’t huge since I quit playing competitively, but I still play in a men’s league—and honestly, it’s a blast. Playing with all the guys is fun, and I’ve met even more lifelong friends through it.

Growing up, I used to dream about starting my own soccer business—creating a place where people could come, hang out, and just play the sport they love.
