About My Skating Passion

I love being active, learning new skills, and challenging myself. Skating—or scootering in my case— is my favorite way to push my limits. Learning new tricks challenges both my mindset and my persistence, and the feeling of finally landing something new is unbeatable.

Skating helps me stay creative and constantly push past my limits. I love coming up with new tricks and lines at the skatepark. My favorite way to skate is to explore the streets and find new spots to ride out in public.

Future

My future in skating is bright because it’s one of my biggest passions, and my whole friend group does it too.

My dream is to get good enough to go out whenever I want and just ride my scooter around, completely free.
